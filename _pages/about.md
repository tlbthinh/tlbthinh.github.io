Hello! I recently completed my M.S. in Computer Science at the University of Dayton, OH, working in the Vision and Mixed Reality Lab with [Prof. Tam Nguyen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=qIaGn7YAAAAJ) on computer vision, multimodal learning, and generative AI. Before that, I spent three years as a backend software engineer at Shopee, an experience that makes me care about methods that hold up outside the lab, not just on benchmarks. My recent work uses generation both as a tool for models — synthesizing adverse weather to stress-test perception — and as a subject of study in itself, from detecting AI-polished reviews to building an open-source AI coding tutor. 

I am open to both research and engineering opportunities where these interests meet.
